Overview

What we built

ITA already has an LMS (Learning Management System) portal for its students. Our contribution is the interactive lessons activity — designed and built by Arignar Tech Solutions and integrated directly into that portal, so the lessons students learn in the classroom can now be practised right here — visually and interactively.

  • Our activity, inside ITA's LMS

    We developed the interactive lessons activity and plugged it seamlessly into ITA's existing learning portal.

  • Learn, then practise

    Everything a student studies in a lesson can be practised right here — visually and interactively.

  • Easy & gamified

    Pictures, audio, progress bars and rewards make concepts simple and keep young learners engaged.

Track 2
Introducing

ITA Tamil Karka

The official mobile app for International Tamil Academy students. We'll be adding many more features to this app over time — for now, we've built two: Word Bank, where kids can look up the meaning of any Tamil word from the lesson they're learning in class, and Announcements, a communication channel where teachers reach parents directly and parents can reply back.

Arignar Tech Solutions· ITA Tamil Karka Track 2 · Mobile App
Feature 1
Word Bank

Word Bank — meanings in your pocket

When a student doesn't understand a word their teacher used in class, they no longer have to wait. They open Word Bank and find the meaning instantly — with a picture and audio to make it clear.

  • Search any word instantly

    Type in a Tamil word and get its meaning right away, or browse vocabulary organized by lesson.

  • Built for grade-school learners

    Covers ITA (KG–Grade 8) and HSCP (Levels 1–4) — the whole learning journey.

  • Learn anywhere

    A lightweight mobile app the diaspora can use at home, on the go, anytime.

Feature 2
Instant Search

Find any word, instantly

Heard a new word in class? Just type a few letters and the meaning is right there.

  • Tamil or English

    Type in either language — the app understands both and finds the right entry.

  • Smart autocomplete

    Even a few letters bring up matching words (வ, வா, வை…) — no perfect spelling needed.

  • Results as you type

    Suggestions appear immediately, so students reach the word they want in seconds.
